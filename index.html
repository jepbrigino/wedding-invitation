What time should we arrive?


Please be there at exactly 4:00 PM. Kindly consider the traffic and travel time.

Can we bring our kids to the wedding?


As much as we love kids, we want the event to be for adults only. The only kids who will be attending are those who are part of the entourage. We are hoping for your kind understanding.

Can I bring someone with me?


Due to space and seating limitations, we kindly request your understanding that only those who have received a personal invitation with an assigned seat will be able to attend. This will help us ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience for all our guests.

Can we take pictures and videos during the ceremony?


Our ceremony is unplugged and we aim to keep it solemn. All guest are requested to refrain from taking pictures/videos during the ceremony. We invited a team to help us capture this day so just be present and keep our ceremony camera-free.

Can we sit anywhere during the reception?


No. It took us a lot of effort and discussion to finish the seating arrangement which is planned for eveyone's convenience and network preference. So, no need to worry. You'll be surely seated along with your group of friends.
